MLSA Youth Empowerment

Who we are?

A vision without a strategy remains an illusion.
At MLSA Youth Empowerment, we don’t just dream we plan and act. Our vision is clear and ambitious.
We aim to bring together Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors and students interested in becoming ambassadors. Our mission is to inform and guide students about the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador program, teaching them the proper usage of Microsoft technologies like Azure.
 We also facilitate interactions with Beta and Gold Ambassadors, as well as Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals (MVPs), empowering students by equipping them with essential Microsoft-related skills.
Join us in making this vision a reality and be part of a community that drives innovation and learning.
